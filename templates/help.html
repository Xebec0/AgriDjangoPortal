Registration Help Guide

How to Register as a Candidate

  1. Click on Register in the navigation menu or on the homepage.
  2. Fill in your username, first name, last name, email, and password.
  3. After registration, you can immediately log in to your account.
  4. Your account will be active right away.

  1. After logging in, go to your Profile page.
  2. Add your biography and location information.
  3. Update your profile information whenever needed.

  1. Visit the Programs page to see available agriculture programs.
  2. Click on a program to view its details.
  3. Use the "Register" button to apply for a program.
  4. Fill in any required notes or additional information.
  5. Submit your registration.

  1. Go to your Profile to view all your program registrations.
  2. Check the status of each registration (Pending, Approved, or Rejected).
  3. You can cancel a pending registration if needed.

Registration Process Guidelines

Document Requirements

  • Valid passport (must be valid for at least 12 months from program start date)
  • Recent professional photo (passport size, white background)
  • CV/Resume with relevant agricultural experience
  • Academic transcripts or certificates
  • Proof of language proficiency (English and/or program-specific languages)
  • Medical clearance form (to be provided during the application process)
  • Program-specific requirements (as specified on the program detail page)

Important Tips

  • Ensure all personal information matches your passport exactly
  • Submit all required documents in clear, legible format
  • Check your application status regularly
  • Respond promptly to any requests for additional information
